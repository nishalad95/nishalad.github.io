Hi I'm Nisha!

I'm an aspiring Data Scientist, Women in STEM advocate & ML engineer based in London. Currently I am in the 3rd year of my PhD, specializing in pattern recognition using GNNs to speed up track finding in the ATLAS detector @ CERN. I have a background in Physics & experience in Software Engineering @ Oracle.
